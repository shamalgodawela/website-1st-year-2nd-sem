FAQs

What is Vathi store?

Online shopping is a type of electronic commerce that allows customers to buy goods or services directly from sellers over the Internet via a web browser or a mobile app. Consumers identify a product of interest by visiting the retailer's website directly or by searching among alternative vendors using a shopping search engine, which compares the availability and cost of the same product across multiple e-retailers.

How can I order?

Using our web platform, you can easily place an order. You can add a product to your cart, login, and complete the purchase procedure once you've found what you're looking for. You will receive an order summary via email once your order is complete. A summary of your order will be saved in your account as well.

Why should I buy online?

The procedure is being sped up. You will be able to acquire rates and go through the order confirmation and payment procedure much faster if you shop online. This might save you days of work.

What information should I input when ordering?

Our online ordering system will prompt you to provide all of the necessary information. Please remember to give your VAT number if you have one. This will ensure that the shipment does not get delayed due to a lack of a VAT number.

What payment methods can I use?

You can use all the major credit cards.

Can I cancel my order?

Please notify us as soon as possible if you wish to cancel your order. If your order has already been processed, you must contact us and return the merchandise. Please send an email to Vathistore@gmail.com

Do you have the product in stock?

All of the products displayed on our website are available. The length of time it takes to complete an order is determined by the products and quantity ordered.
