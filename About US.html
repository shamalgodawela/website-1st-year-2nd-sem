Our Small Business Accelerator Program: Up & Running

Our Up & Running initiative assists small businesses in getting online and growing their enterprises. We provided fee discounts, customized coaching, and resources to help small businesses run their Vathi stores under the program. The idea has been adapted in over 25 countries around the world, and it has now expanded its efforts with a new grants program, in which 50 small company sellers in the United States got $10,000 in packages.


We put resources and strategies in place to help retailers succeed in ecommerce now and in the future. Our Vathi Seller School is a new virtual learning platform that provides on-demand lectures and videos on a wide range of ecommerce topics critical to digital economy success. Image Clean-Up, Time Away, and Seller Initiated Offers are just a few of the tools we provide to help sellers accelerate and develop their eBay businesses.
